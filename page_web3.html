How Governance Works

  • 1

    Proposal Creation

    Any community member can submit a proposal to the DAO.

  • 2

    Community Discussion

    The community reviews and discusses proposals.

  • 3

    Voting by VYX Holders

    VYX holders vote using their voting power.

  • 4

    Execution

    Approved proposals are executed on-chain.

  • 5

    Results & Transparency

    All actions and results are transparent and verifiable.
